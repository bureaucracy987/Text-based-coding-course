Babur, the Mughal emperor

Name Zahir-ud-din Muhammad Babur
Born in 14 February 1483
Mother Qutlugh Nigar Khanum
Father Umar Sheikh Mirza
Achievements Babur conquered Kabul in Afghanistan as a young man, and later claimed Northern India after the First Battle of Panipat.In 1526, Babur defeated the last leader of the Delhi Sultanate in the First Battle of Panipat. Babur defeated the Rajputi Hindu princes in the Battle of Khanwa, near Fatehpur Sikri. He then went on to subdue the Rajputs of Chanderi.
Died on 26 December 1530
